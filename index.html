Biography Resume

I am now a research engineer at SII, working on humanoid intelligence.

I earned my M.S. in 2024 from the NLP group at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xipeng Qiu. During this period, I was fortunate to be mentored by Prof. Hongyuan Mei and Prof. Matthew R. Walter in the robotics lab at the TTIC, and I also spent some time at the Shanghai AI Lab and an AI virtual human startup. Before that, I completed my B.S. from the School of Data Science and Engineering at the ECNU, under the guidance of Prof. Yuanbin Wu.
